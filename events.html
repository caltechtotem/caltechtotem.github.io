Events


Join us every first Sunday of the month from 5-8pm in the Sherman Fairchild Library (SFL) Room 326 for a creative writing session to work on your awesome short story, poem, or novel.
Pizza and soft drinks will be provided. Don't forget to bring a laptop or pen + paper to write with! E-mail totem@caltech.edu if you have any questions.
Hope to see you there!
